Dutch Colonial Heritage Datahub

The Dutch Colonial Heritage Datahub is a place to explore for objects that have their origins in former Dutch colonial countries like Suriname, Indonesia, Sri Lanka, South Afrika, The Dutch Antiles and many more countries. You can add your own narrative to these objects and tell your own story.

Herritage communities

Every culture can start its own community on this platform. A community can create lists of objects that tells stories about their history. Communities can also add narratives to the objects in this collection.
