Dutch Colonial Collection Datahub

The Dutch Colonial Collection Datahub is a place to explore for objects that are currently located in the Netherlands but have their origins in former Dutch colonial countries like Suriname, Indonesia, Sri Lanka, South Afrika, The Dutch Antiles and many more countries. See how this works.

Add your narrative to the objects

Your knowledge about the objects is vital to us. With your stories we can put these objects in their original context.

When you create an account you can tell your story about these objects, use communities and create lists.

Herritage communities

Every culture can start its own community on this platform. A community can create lists of objects that tells stories about their history. Communities can also add narratives to the objects in this collection.
