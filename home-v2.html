Objects

Add your narratives and provenance to objects

Your knowledge about the heritage on this platform is vital. With your narratives and perspectives, we can present the ‘objects’ in their context and point to biases in collection metadata.

When you create an account, you can add your narrative to these ‘objects’, make communities and create lists.

Communities

Be part of a community

Upon creating an account, every individual or group of people can start their own community on this platform. A community is able to create lists of objects, bringing together their heritage. Communities can also add narratives to the objects in these collections.

Lists

Create and share lists

You can group object to create thematic lists.

Provenance Research Guide

The provenance Research Guide is a tool to help you do research specialised in a colonial context. There’s introduction section to get you started into the the research. Furthermore there are sections diving deeper into topics like trade, marine, militaire and Civil servants.

The Colonial Collections DataHub is a project of the Colonial Collections Consortium.

The consortium is a partnership between five organizations with expertise in the field of colonial collections.

Dutch Colonial Collection Datahub

The Dutch Colonial Collection Datahub is a place to explore for objects that are currently located in the Netherlands but have their origins in former Dutch colonial countries like Suriname, Indonesia, Sri Lanka, South Afrika, The Dutch Antiles and many more countries. See how this works.
